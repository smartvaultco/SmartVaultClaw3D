## GHL SETUP (Step by Step)

### Step 1: Get the List
- Ask client for CSV/Excel of past customers (name + phone minimum, email is bonus)
- Minimum 50 contacts, ideally 200+
- Must be their own customers (not purchased lists — compliance)

### Step 2: Import to GHL
- Go to client's sub-account → Contacts → Import
- Map fields: First Name, Last Name, Phone, Email
- Apply tag: `DBR-Campaign`

### Step 3: Build the Automation
**Trigger:** Tag `DBR-Campaign` applied

**Message 1 — SMS (Immediate):**
```
Hey [FIRST NAME]! It's [BUSINESS NAME] 👋 

We haven't talked in a while and wanted to reach out with something special — we're offering [OFFER] for past customers only.

This week only. Want to book? Reply YES and I'll get you scheduled.
```

**Message 2 — Email (4 hours later):**
```
Subject: [FIRST NAME], exclusive offer from [BUSINESS NAME]

Hi [FIRST NAME],

Since you're one of our valued past customers, we wanted to give you first access to a special offer:

🎉 [OFFER DETAILS — e.g., "Free roof inspection + 15% off any repair this month"]

This is only available to past customers and expires [DATE].

Reply to this email or click here to book: [BOOKING LINK]

Thanks,
[BUSINESS NAME] Team
```

**Message 3 — SMS (24 hours after Message 1, only if no reply):**
```
Just following up, [FIRST NAME] — did you see our exclusive offer for past customers? 

[SHORT OFFER REMINDER]

Spots are filling up. Reply YES to grab yours before [DATE].
```

**Message 4 — SMS (48 hours, final push, only if no reply):**
```
Last chance, [FIRST NAME] — our [OFFER] for past customers ends [DATE]. 

After that it goes back to regular price. Want in? Reply YES.
```

### Step 4: Monitor & Route
- Replies auto-tagged `DBR-Replied`
- Positive replies → move to pipeline stage "Qualified"
- Notify client via email/SMS when someone books
- Track: messages sent, reply rate, appointments booked, revenue generated
