### Google 100 + ChatGPT Scraping
1. Install "Google 100 results" Chrome extension
2. Search: "[niche] in [city]" + "@gmail.com" or "WhatsApp"
3. Copy all 100 results → paste into ChatGPT
4. Prompt: "Extract business names, emails, phone numbers into a CSV. Filter out any with professional websites."
5. Import CSV into GHL Contacts
