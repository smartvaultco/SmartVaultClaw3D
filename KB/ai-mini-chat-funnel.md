## Core Framework
Automated system capturing leads and delivering value. "Rinse and Repeat" — clone successful funnels for multiple income streams.

### Customer Journey
1. Visitor lands → Chat greets → Options shown
2. User selects → Data collected → Resource delivered
3. Thank you/next steps → Follow-up emails/SMS
4. Upsell/cross-sell → Ongoing engagement

---

## 12-Step SOP

### Phase 1: Foundation
1. **Choose Niche & Offer:** Brainstorm 3-5 niches (health, business, beauty, real estate) → pick ONE niche + ONE lead magnet
2. **Select Tools:** Chatbot + Email/SMS + Tracking + optional LMS
3. **Plan Customer Journey:** Basic options: Get free resource / Ask question / Book a call

### Phase 2: Build
4. **Build Mini Chat:** Script all messages (Welcome, Options, Lead Capture, Delivery). Configure auto-replies, email sync, tagging. TEST all flows yourself
5. **Connect Integrations:** Email/SMS platform, tracking pixels, LMS/delivery
6. **Create Lead Magnet:** PDF/video/checklist/Google Doc. 7-part outline: Cover, Intro, Step 1, Step 2, Step 3, Resources, Next Step/CTA

### Phase 3: Launch & Scale
7. **Launch & Test:** Embed chat widget on site/landing page. Test as user
8. **Follow-Up Automations:** 4-part Email/SMS: Delivery → Quick Win → Value & Pitch → Testimonial/Case Study
9. **Drive Traffic:** Social posts (IG, TikTok, FB, LinkedIn), paid ads (Meta, Google, TikTok), email list, forums, partnerships
10. **Track & Improve:** Monitor opens/clicks/conversions, adjust copy/offers, duplicate for new niches
11. **Upsell/Cross-Sell:** Paid product invite, discount, referral incentive
12. **Rinse & Repeat:** Save scripts/flows/assets → clone for new income streams

## Tool Directory
- **Chatbots:** ManyChat, Tidio, Chatbase, Landbot, Intercom
- **Email/SMS:** Mailchimp, Klaviyo, Brevo
- **Tracking:** Google Analytics, Meta Pixel
- **LMS/Delivery:** Teachable, Thinkific, Google Classroom, Notion, Google Drive
- **Content Creation:** ChatGPT, Canva

## Chatbot Scripts
- Welcome: "Hey! Want a free [resource]? Pick one: Get the free guide / Ask a question / Book a call"
- Lead Capture: "Awesome! Drop your best email to get your free [resource]."
- Delivery: "Here's your guide! Check your inbox. Want more help? [Next steps]"

## Email Scripts
- Email 1 (Delivery): "Here's Your Free [Guide]... Download here: [link]"
- Email 2 (Quick Win): "Quick Tip for You... Here's a tip to get the most out of your [guide]"
- Email 3 (Value & Pitch): Offer call or paid product
- Email 4 (Social Proof): Testimonial/case study
