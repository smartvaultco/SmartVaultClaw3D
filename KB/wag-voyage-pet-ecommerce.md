## Core E-Commerce Strategy
- Product viability: strong marketplace traction + robust suppliers + clear consumer demand
- Optimal price range: $10-$39 (impulse purchases + bundling)
- Review pain point analysis: durability, sizing, cleaning difficulty → informs supplier negotiations
- Landed cost formula: supplier_price + supplier_shipping + estimated customs/taxes

## Risk Management
- **Seasonality:** Enrichment peaks Feb, travel peaks summer
- **Regulatory:** FDA (food/supplements), FCC (batteries), cosmetic standards (wipes), compostable standards (poop bags)
- **Logistics:** Bulky/fragile items need special packaging + higher shipping costs
