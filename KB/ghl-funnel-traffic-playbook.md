## TRAFFIC STRATEGY

### ORGANIC (Free — Do This First)

#### LinkedIn (Primary for "Pat" audience)
- **Post 3-5x/week** — AI automation tips, mini case studies, hot takes
- **Format mix:** Text posts, carousels (Canva), short video clips
- **Content source:** Pull directly from ebook chapters (you already wrote 40K words)
- **CTA:** "I wrote the full breakdown in my free AI Playbook → [link in comments]"
- **DMs:** Value-first outreach to SMB owners. No pitching in first message.

#### YouTube (Long-form authority)
- **1 video/week** — Film the course module content, post publicly
- **Title formula:** Use your ATP questions as video titles (already search-validated)
- **Description:** Link to lead magnet
- **Shorts:** Chop each video into 3-5 shorts (60 sec clips of best moments)

#### TikTok / Instagram Reels
- **Repurpose YouTube Shorts** — same clips, cross-post
- **Hook-heavy:** First 3 seconds must be a pattern interrupt
- **CTA:** "Link in bio" → Linktree or GHL landing page

#### SEO / Blog
- **Publish ebook chapters as blog posts** on your website (with lead magnet CTA embedded)
- **JSON-LD schema markup** for AI Overviews / SGE (you already know this from your KB)
- **Answer-first structure** at top of every post

#### Email List
- Every new lead enters the nurture sequence
- Weekly newsletter (repurpose LinkedIn posts + new insights)
- Segment: Free downloaders vs. ebook buyers vs. course students

### PAID ADS (Scale After Organic Proves the Message)

#### When to Start Ads
- After organic generates 50+ leads (proves the message converts)
- After you have at least 2-3 testimonials/results

#### Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
**Budget:** Start at $20-50/day per ad set

**Campaign Structure:**
```
CAMPAIGN: AI Growth Playbook — Lead Gen
  ├── AD SET 1: SMB Owners (Interest targeting)
  │     ├── Ad 1: Pain-point hook (video, 30 sec)
  │     ├── Ad 2: Case study hook (video, 45 sec)  
  │     └── Ad 3: Stat hook (image + text)
  ├── AD SET 2: Entrepreneurs (Lookalike)
  │     └── [Same 3 ad variants]
  └── AD SET 3: Retargeting (visited page, didn't opt in)
        └── Testimonial / social proof ad
```

**Ad Creative (Use New Standard Ad Framework):**
- **Hook (0-7 sec):** "I replaced 40 hours of manual work with AI in one afternoon."
- **Agitate (7-15 sec):** "Most business owners are still doing everything by hand — posting manually, answering every DM, running ads blind."
- **Solution + Proof (15-35 sec):** "I built an AI system that handles it all. Here's what happened in 60 days..." [show results]
- **CTA (35-45 sec):** "Download the free playbook — link below."

**Kill Rules (SOP-004):**
- Kill ad at 72 hours if CTR < 0.7% AND zero leads
- Scale ad +30% budget if CPL < target for 72 hours

#### Google Ads
**Budget:** $30-50/day
**Type:** Search ads targeting ATP keywords
- "AI automation for small business"
- "how to automate my business with AI"
- "AI marketing tools for small business"
**Landing page:** Lead magnet page

#### TikTok Ads
**Budget:** $20-30/day
**Type:** Spark Ads (boost organic posts that performed well)
**Targeting:** Business owners, entrepreneurs, marketing managers
