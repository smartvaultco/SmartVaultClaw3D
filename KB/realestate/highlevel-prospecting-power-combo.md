## Core Framework: The Power Combo

Automated local business prospecting machine running 24/7/365. Instead of cold pitching marketing services, act as a community builder offering a free local directory listing. This "Trojan Horse" starts low-friction conversations, uses software automations to upsell, and leverages automated marketing audits to pivot into high-ticket service sales.

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Account Setup
- Upgrade HighLevel to Premium Prospecting version
- Enable bulk prospecting in "Settings and Labs"
- Link premium subscription to a specific sub-account

### Step 2: Prospect Discovery
- Open HighLevel prospecting tool within sub-account
- Search for a niche (e.g., "plumbers") in a specific location

### Step 3: Bypassing Google's 60-Business Cap
- Change search radius (e.g., 3.1 miles)
- Alter search terms (e.g., "emergency plumbers," "electricians")
- Pull 120-500 targeted leads in under 5 minutes

### Step 4: Bulk Import & Audit Generation
- "Add multiple prospects" button imports into sub-account in bulk
- System auto-generates marketing audit for each business (Google Business Profile, website, reviews, AI chat widget, local SEO heat map)

### Step 5: Trigger Free Offer
- Navigate to contacts tab
- Select imported businesses
- Apply tag "business"
- Smart Directory AI auto-creates free listing on localized directory site

### Step 6: Automated Outreach & Upsell
- Auto-email notifying business of free listing
- When they reply to confirm → pitch paid premium listing upgrade
- Invite to strategy call for more exposure

### Step 7: The Courtesy Call & Pivot
- "Courtesy call" to review premium listing setup
- Pivot by offering marketing audit report as free bonus

### Step 8: Pitching Solutions
- Use audit report to highlight gaps:
  - Missing AI chat widget → pitch 24/7 automated chat
  - Poor SEO heat map rankings → pitch local SEO services
