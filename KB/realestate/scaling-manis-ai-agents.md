## Core Frameworks

### General Contractor Model (Arbitrage)
- Sell digital marketing services without doing the work yourself
- Find client → collect fee ($1K) → outsource to Upwork specialists ($300) → keep profit ($700)
- You are "general contractors on the internet using AI as tool of choice"

### Foot-in-the-Door Subscription
- Sell pre-built template website for $500 (anchored from $1K)
- Once trust built → upsell monthly recurring: SEO, Google Ads, social media ($500-$12K/mo)

### Agentic Parallel Processing (Wide Research)
- Manus AI deploys up to 100 sub-agents simultaneously
- 10 hours of work → 10 minutes. Generate 50 poster designs or analyze 100 competitors in parallel

## 7 Client Acquisition Strategies (Manus AI Deep Research)

1. **Ultra-Custom Pitches:** Extract prospect's website performance/SEO gaps/ad presence → hyper-personalized cold outreach
2. **Competitor Gap Analysis:** Audit prospect's competitor → find missing keywords → pitch 10-min breakdown
3. **Lead Magnet Audits:** AI-generated "Local Business Visibility Reports" → offer free via DMs/email
4. **Social Media Interception:** Scrape Facebook groups/LinkedIn for owners asking for marketing help → instant AI-generated reply
5. **Reviving Old Leads:** Deep research on ghosted prospects for fresh issues (Yelp rating drop) → re-engage hook
6. **Weekly "Spy Reports":** Post public teardowns of local businesses → attract inbound leads
7. **Objection-Proof Sales Scripts:** AI identifies bad reviews/weak CTAs/outdated content → pitch exact fix before they ask
