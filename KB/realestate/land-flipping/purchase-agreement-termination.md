## LEGAL CLAUSES & CONTRACT LANGUAGE

### Mutual Release & Waiver Clause
> "NOW THEREFORE, with each party's signature herein, he/she/they unconditionally waive and release any claim against each other arising from the Agreement or by reason of its termination."

**Key Points:**
- Both parties must sign to activate waiver
- Unconditional release of all claims related to original agreement
- Applies to claims arising from agreement OR its termination

### Escrow & Deposit Distribution Clause
> "FUTHERMORE, the Buyer and Seller hereby agree that any deposit, earnest money, or any other monies held by any real estate agent, attorney, third (3rd) party, or any individual or entity holding funds on an escrow basis shall be distributed in the following amounts to the following parties:"

**Distribution Template:**
```
$__________________ to __________________
$__________________ to __________________
$__________________ to __________________
```

**Handling Instructions:**
- Identify ALL parties holding funds (agents, attorneys, escrow holders)
- Specify exact dollar amounts for each distribution
- Name recipient for each distribution
- Must be mutually agreed upon before termination is final

## KEY PROCESS: TERMINATING A PURCHASE AGREEMENT

### Step-by-Step Checklist

- [ ] **Obtain original purchase agreement** — Verify effective date and parties
- [ ] **Confirm all deposit/earnest money locations** — Identify all entities holding funds
- [ ] **Negotiate fund distribution** — Align buyer and seller on split amounts
- [ ] **Complete party information** — Fill all blank fields (names, dates, amounts)
- [ ] **Prepare escrow distribution schedule** — Document exact amounts and recipients
- [ ] **Obtain buyer signature(s)** — Both parties if joint ownership/multiple buyers
- [ ] **Obtain seller signature(s)** — Both parties if joint ownership/multiple sellers
- [ ] **Obtain agent signature(s)** — If agents are holding earnest money
- [ ] **Obtain third-party signatures** — Attorney, escrow company, or other fund holders
- [ ] **Verify all dates** — Ensure consistency across all signatures
- [ ] **Distribute to escrow holder** — Provide signed document to funds holder
- [ ] **Obtain written confirmation** — Fund distribution receipt from escrow holder
- [ ] **File in records** — Store termination agreement with original purchase agreement

## CRITICAL WARNINGS & NON-NEGOTIABLES

### Do NOT Proceed Without:
❌ State-specific attorney review  
❌ Verification of all fund holders and amounts  
❌ Written agreement on deposit distribution from ALL parties  
❌ All required signatures from buyer, seller, and agents  

### Common Issues to Avoid:
- Using this template across multiple states without modification
- Incomplete signatory lines (missing agents or third-party holders)
- Ambiguous or missing fund distribution amounts
- No documentation of where deposits are currently held
