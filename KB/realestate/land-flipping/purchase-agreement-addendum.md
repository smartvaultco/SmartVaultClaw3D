## ADDENDUM TO PURCHASE AGREEMENT — TEMPLATE

### Document Structure

#### I. PARTIES SECTION
```
Effective Date: _________________, 20____

The Parties. This Addendum hereby becomes part of the Purchase Agreement 
between _________________ ("Buyer") and _________________ ("Seller") 
authorized on _________________, 20____ ("Agreement") for the property 
located at _________________, City of _________________, 
State of _________________ ("Property").
```

**Required Fields to Complete:**
1. Effective date (MM/DD/YYYY format recommended)
2. Buyer name and role designation
3. Seller name and role designation
4. Original agreement date
5. Property street address
6. City
7. State

#### II. AMENDMENT(S) SECTION
```
Amendment(s). Buyer and Seller agree that the Agreement shall be amended 
as follows: _______________________________________________________________
______________________________________________________________________
______________________________________________________________________
______________________________________________________________________
______________________________________________________________________

All other terms and conditions of the Agreement shall remain the same. 
This Addendum shall go into effect on the date of the undersigned parties.
```

**Usage:** Insert specific amendments, modifications, or conditions that modify the original Purchase Agreement.
