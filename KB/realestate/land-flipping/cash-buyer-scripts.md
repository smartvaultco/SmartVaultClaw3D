## CORE STRATEGIES & FRAMEWORKS

### Cash Buyer List Development Strategy
**Objective**: Build a database of active investors with liquid funds and deal criteria

**Primary Methods**:
1. Identify investors through property records and transaction history
2. Research investor backgrounds via agent contacts
3. Qualify buyers on specific criteria before adding to list
4. Segment buyers by deal type and geographic preference
5. Implement multi-touch communication sequence (email → SMS → voicemail)

### Deal Discovery & Sourcing
- Find properties in areas where known investors have recently purchased
- Identify contractors working on renovation projects (investor indicator)
- Monitor rental listings to find active investor-landlords
- Use agent networks to identify flipper activity

### Contact Acquisition Methods
1. **Agent Route**: Contact listing agents who represent investors
2. **Contractor Route**: Get investor contact info from contractors on renovation sites
3. **Rental Property Route**: Call on advertised rentals; pivot to investment inquiry
4. **Public Records Route**: Research recent investor purchases

### SMS SCRIPTS

#### First Contact Script — Adding Cash Buyers
> Hi _____. This is ______. I often get off-market discounted properties in your area...

**Follow-up Questions (Ask 1-2)**:
- Are you looking to buy an investment property?
- What types of properties do you look for?
- Can I let you know when I get my next discounted property?
- Can I add you to my buyer's list?

#### SMS Blast Template — Texting Wholesale Deals
**Format**:
1. **First Line (Grab Attention)**: "New Deal!..." / "Hot Deal!..." / "Just Acquired!..."
2. **Address**: Example: 123 Main St, Mesa
3. **Price/EMD**: Example: $95k Cash, $2500 EMD
4. **Property Details**: Sqft, Bds/baths, lot size, yr built, occupied/vacant, outstanding features
5. **Closing Date**: Date on contract with seller
6. **Showing Instructions**: Call to schedule or Open Inspection on ______ (date/time)
7. **Picture Link**: Dropbox, Google Drive, website, etc.

### VOICEMAIL SCRIPTS

#### Version 1 — Specific Deal Pitch
> Hi sorry I missed you. I believe you're a real estate investor in the area and I was calling about a distressed investment property I need to sell quickly on ____________[street/city] and just wanted to reach out and see if you might have any interest in buying it. I can tell you all the details and if you're interested, we can set up a time for you to see it.
>
> And listen… if I got the wrong person or you're not interested I'm sorry for the interruption but give me a shout back even if you're not thinking about buying right now just so I know to take you off our list. You can reach me directly at ___________. Look forward to talking to you soon.
>
> Again the number to reach me is ______________.

#### Version 2 — Vague Deal Inquiry (Softer Approach)
> Hi, sorry I missed you. I realize this is kind of a random call but the reason I'm calling is because I believe you're an investor and we recently acquired a property in your area and I wanted to see if you would also be interested in buying it.
>
> If not that's fine, you can just disregard this call but we're looking to sell it at a discount right away to an investor. Anyway, if that's something you're interested in…I can get you details. Let me know, give me a call back at ________.
>
> Again, if you're really serious about giving an offer, please call me back at _________

#### Version 3 — List Building Voicemail
> Hi sorry I missed you. Real quick, the reason for the call is I'm a local investor and we often get off-market discounted properties in your area and I wanted to ask if you were currently looking to buy an investment property and what types of properties you look for?
>
> And listen if I got the wrong person or you're not interested, I'm sorry for the interruption but if you are, give me a shout back and let me know when you're looking for your next deal so I can I add you to my buyer's list.
>
> You can reach me directly at ___________. Look forward to talking to you soon. Again the number to reach me is__________.

### CONTRACTOR CONTACT SCRIPTS

#### Getting Investor Contact Info from Contractor
> Hi I'm a local investor and I buy and fix properties to rent or flip and I'm looking for a good contractor to do work on my houses. Do you have a business card..?
>
> …By the way, I may have a deal that your boss (investor) may be interested in, can you give me his/her contact info so I can call him/her?

#### Calling Property "For Rent" Script
**Initial Voicemail**:
> Hi I'm calling about the house for rent at _______(address). Give me a call back at ________(your number).

**When Investor Calls Back**:
> I'm not calling about renting but rather I'm an investor and we're looking to buy a property in this area and I wanted to see if you were interested in selling…?

**If "No" Response**:
> Ok, no problem…BTW - I pick up a lot of discounted properties, are you looking for more properties? What type of deals are you looking for? What's your price range? If I find a deal that meets your criteria, would you buy it?

## CASH BUYER QUALIFICATION FRAMEWORK

### 10 Essential Questions for Every Cash Buyer

1. **Deal Type**: Are you looking for buy and hold or fix and flip deals?
2. **Geographic Focus**: What areas do you like to buy in?
3. **Property Criteria**: What types of property criteria do you require?
4. **Return Expectations**: What type of return are you typically looking for? (What is your buy formula?)
5. **Capital Source**: Do you purchase properties with liquid funds or do you borrow capital?
6. **Closing Timeline**: How much time do you require to close on deals?
7. **Volume Capacity**: How many per month can you take down?
8. **Deal Readiness**: When are you looking to close on your next deal?
9. **Additional Services**: What other services can I help you with?
10. **Protocol Agreement**: Are you willing to follow my protocol for going under contract on deals?

## SMS BLASTING COMPLIANCE & BEST PRACTICES

### Anti-Detection Rules (Carrier/Legal Compliance)
- **Rotation**: Rotate through 5-10 different message versions
- **Personalization**: State recipient name AND your name
- **Clarity**: Be clear about your intentions
- **Conversation**: Be ready to have a two-way conversation
- **Solicitation**: Don't sell or solicit products and services
- **CTA**: Always end with a question
- **DNC List**: Don't message DNC (Do Not Call) list
- **Litigator List**: Don't message litigators list
- **Character Limit**: Keep under 160 characters

## CONTRACTOR DISCOVERY WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process
1. **Identify** properties under active renovation in target area
2. **Approach** contractor on-site
3. **Build rapport** by discussing contractor services
4. **Request** business card or contact info
5. **Ask directly** for investor/owner contact information
6. **Position deal** as potential investment opportunity
7. **Follow up** with investor directly
