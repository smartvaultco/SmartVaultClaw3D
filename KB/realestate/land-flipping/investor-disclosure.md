## 4. INVESTOR DISCLOSURE ADDENDUM

### Document Structure
- **Parties:** BUYER, SELLER, END-BUYER (third-party assignee)
- **Required Fields:**
  - BUYER name: ___________
  - SELLER name: ___________
  - Date authorized: ___________, 20____
  - PROPERTY address: _____________________
  - State of license (if applicable): _____

### 4.1 EQUITY RELEASE CLAUSE

#### Purpose
Protects buyer from seller claims regarding below-market-value purchases

#### Required Seller Acknowledgment
> SELLER acknowledges that the purchase price may be less than market value and voluntarily agreed to sell PROPERTY to BUYER for convenience, to save time and/or money, lack of funds to renovate, and/or any other personal reasons and herein releases and waives any/all claims against existing or future equity arising from PROPERTY.

#### Buyer Representation Disclaimer
> BUYER has not made SELLER any representations or promises as to the value of PROPERTY in its "as is" current condition.

#### Key Elements
- Price may be **below market value** — seller acknowledges voluntarily
- Accepted reasons for below-market sale:
  - Convenience
  - Time savings
  - Cost savings
  - Lack of renovation funds
  - Other personal reasons
- **SELLER waives all claims** against current and future equity

---

### 4.2 PROFIT DISCLOSURE CLAUSE

#### Purpose
Ensures seller knows buyer is an investor seeking profit, not a retail buyer

#### Required Language
> PROFIT DISCLOSURE: SELLER acknowledges that BUYER is an investor and is purchasing PROPERTY with the intent to make a profit by either renting, fixing and flipping, re-selling and/or assigning the AGREEMENT (wholesaling).

#### Buyer Intent Options (Disclosure)
- **Renting** (rental income strategy)
- **Fixing and flipping** (renovate and resell)
- **Re-selling** (direct resale without renovation)
- **Assigning the agreement** (wholesaling)

---

### 4.3 ASSIGNMENT DISCLOSURE CLAUSE

#### Purpose
Alerts seller that buyer may transfer contract to third-party (end-buyer) before closing

#### Required Language
> ASSIGNMENT DISCLOSURE: SELLER acknowledges that BUYER may Assign his/her interest in the purchase of PROPERTY to a third-party "END-BUYER," who shall be any party selected at any time by BUYER prior to Close of Escrow (COE) and affirmed with a separate written Assignment of Agreement executed by BUYER and END-BUYER.

#### Assignment Fee Disclosure
> In the event of an Assignment of Agreement, SELLER acknowledges that BUYER will collect a fee from END-BUYER that is separate from the purchase price in the AGREEMENT.

#### Key Terms
- **Timing:** Third-party can be selected any time prior to Close of Escrow (COE)
- **Documentation:** Requires separate written Assignment of Agreement
- **Parties:** Must be executed by BUYER and END-BUYER
- **Fee Structure:** BUYER collects fee from END-BUYER (not from SELLER)
- **Fee Separation:** Fee is **separate and distinct** from original purchase price

---

### 4.4 AGENCY DISCLOSURE CLAUSE

#### Purpose
Clarifies buyer is acting as principal, not as seller's agent/broker

#### License Status Checkbox
```
AGENCY DISCLOSURE. SELLER acknowledges that BUYER:
____ DOES NOT hold an active real estate license with the state of _____.
____ Holds an active real estate license with the state of ______.
```

#### Required Seller Acknowledgment
> SELLER acknowledges that SELLER has NOT been represented by BUYER or by any representative of BUYER with respect to the AGREEMENT.

#### Buyer's Role Definition
> SELLER agrees and understands that the BUYER or BUYER'S Representatives are NOT acting as SELLER'S Broker or Agent in the transaction and is acting solely for BUYER'S own benefit as a principal to this AGREEMENT.

#### Liability Release
> SELLER agrees to hold BUYER free from any/all liability regarding the PROPERTY and transaction arising from any claim of agency.

#### Key Elements
- Buyer acts as **principal, not agent**
- Buyer is **not seller's broker**
- Buyer may or may not hold real estate license (disclosed)
- Seller receives **no agent representation**
- Buyer acts **solely for own benefit**
- Seller **releases all agency claims** against buyer

## 6. CRITICAL PROCESS: DISCLOSURE IMPLEMENTATION

### Sequential Steps for Purchase Agreement
1. **Prepare Main Purchase Agreement** with standard terms (price, property, earnest money)
2. **Attach Appropriate Addendums** based on transaction type:
   - All transactions: EQUITY RELEASE + PROFIT DISCLOSURE + AGENCY DISCLOSURE
   - Wholesaling deals: Add ASSIGNMENT DISCLOSURE
3. **Complete All Fill-in Fields:**
   - Buyer name
   - Seller name
   - Date authorized
   - Property address
   - State (for agency license disclosure)
4. **Seller Acknowledgment:** Ensure seller reviews and understands each disclosure
5. **Execute Signatures:** Both buyer and seller sign and date all addendums
6. **Document Retention:** Keep executed copies for compliance/dispute protection
