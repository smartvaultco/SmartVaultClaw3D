## ESCALATION CLAUSE FRAMEWORK

### Document Purpose
**Type:** Addendum to Purchase Agreement  
**Usage:** For competitive multi-offer situations where Buyer wants automatic price adjustment  
**Status:** For use only by authorized members

---

## ESCALATION CLAUSE STRUCTURE

### Section 1: ESCALATION TERMS

#### 1.A — Escalating Factor (Core Mechanism)

**Trigger Conditions:**
- Seller receives one or more additional bona fide offers to purchase property
- Other offers have terms acceptable to Seller
- Other offers result in net proceeds equal to or greater than original Purchase Agreement

**Automatic Action:**
> In the event that Seller receives one or more additional bona fide offers to purchase the Property with terms acceptable to Seller ("Other Offers"), but which result in net proceeds of sale payable to the Seller equal to or greater than the net proceeds of sale payable to the Seller under this Purchase Agreement, then the sale price stated in this Purchase Agreement shall automatically increase to an amount which generates net proceeds of sale to Seller equal to $ _____________, in US Dollars, ("Escalating Factor") in excess of the highest net proceeds of sale generated in such Other Offers.

**Formula:**
```
New Purchase Price = Highest Net Proceeds from Other Offers + Escalating Factor ($)
```

**Blank Fields to Complete:**
- `$_____________` = Escalating Factor amount (dollar amount above competing offers)

---

#### 1.B — Price Cap (Non-Negotiable Ceiling)

> The purchase price under this Purchase Agreement shall not exceed $ ___________, in US Dollars ("Cap").

**Key Rule:**
- Escalation clause CANNOT exceed the Cap amount, regardless of competing offers
- Blank field: `$__________` = Maximum purchase price ceiling

---

#### 1.C — Documentation Requirement

> In the event that Other Offers cause the escalation of the purchase price in this Purchase Agreement the Seller will provide the Buyer with sufficient documentation to justify the purchase price increase.

**Buyer Right:**
- Request and receive proof/documentation of competing offers justifying price increase

---

#### 1.D — Multiple Escalations Acknowledgment

> The Buyer acknowledges that the Escalating Factor of this Purchase Agreement and the Escalating Factors of Other Offers may result in multiple escalations, and in some cases, escalation to the Cap.

**Scenario Acceptance:**
- Buyer accepts that multiple rounds of escalation are possible
- Price may reach the Cap limit

---

### Section 2: ESCALATION INCREASE PROCEDURE (Financing Options)

**Condition:** Applies only if Buyer is financing a portion of purchase price AND escalation occurs

**Option A: Keep Loan Same, Pay Increase in Cash**
```
[ ] The loan amount provided for in this Purchase Agreement shall remain the same, 
    and Buyer shall pay any increase in cash at the time of closing
```
**Impact:** Down payment increases; total cash outlay increases

---

**Option B: Keep Down Payment Same, Add Increase to Loan**
```
[ ] The Down Payment amount provided for in this Purchase Agreement shall remain 
    the same, and any increase shall be added to the loan amount
```
**Impact:** Loan amount increases; monthly payment increases

---

**Option C: Loan Increases as Percentage of New Price**
```
[ ] The loan amount provided for in this Purchase Agreement shall automatically 
    increase to be _______ % of the new purchase price of the Property
```
**Blank Field:** `_______` = Loan-to-Value percentage (e.g., 80%)  
**Impact:** Both loan and down payment increase proportionally

---

**Option D: Loan with Maximum Cap**
```
[ ] The loan amount shall not exceed $ ________ in US Dollars and Buyer shall pay 
    any amount of the increase in purchase price which is not included in the loan 
    amount in cash at the time of closing
```
**Blank Fields:**
- `$________` = Maximum loan amount
- Buyer pays excess in cash

**Impact:** Controls loan exposure; requires cash reserves for overages

---

### Section 3: TERMINATION

> The terms contained herein are for the use and purpose of obtaining a mutually agreeable purchase price and shall be deemed satisfied and will terminate upon ratification of the modified terms of this Purchase Agreement for the above captioned property.

**When Escalation Ends:**
- Once final price is agreed and counter-offer is signed
- Escalation clause becomes null after ratification

---

### Section 4: BROKER LIABILITY WAIVER

> Buyer acknowledges and affirms this Purchase Agreement and Escalation Clause has been made of his/her own volition and at his/her own discretion and Buyer agrees to hold Brokers harmless with regard to negotiation of the purchase price.

**Requirement:**
- Buyer assumes full responsibility for escalation clause
- Cannot hold brokers liable for price negotiation consequences

---

### Section 5: COUNTER OFFER PROCESS

> In the event that Seller agrees to accept a purchase price within the terms of this Purchase Agreement and pursuant to this Escalation Clause, Seller will submit to Buyer a Counter Offer and a copy of this Addendum, with the purchase price adjusted according to the escalation provisions contained herein.

**Procedure:**
1. Seller receives competing offer(s)
2. Seller submits Counter Offer to Buyer with updated purchase price
3. Updated Addendum included showing new price calculation
4. Buyer signs modified Counter Offer
5. Fully executed Counter Offer delivered to Seller
6. Agreement becomes binding

**Final Signature Requirement:**
> Acceptance of the Counter Offer will occur upon counter signature of the modified terms of this Purchase Agreement, by Buyer, and delivery to Seller of the fully executed Counter Offer.

## KEY OPERATIONAL RULES

### Non-Negotiables

1. **Attorney Consultation Mandatory**
   - All real estate contracts require state-specific attorney review
   - Do not duplicate without legal guidance

2. **Documentation Required**
   - Seller must provide proof of competing offers justifying price increases
   - Buyer has right to review documentation

3. **Cap is Absolute Ceiling**
   - Purchase price cannot exceed the Cap amount under any circumstances
   - This is the hard stop for escalation

4. **Financing Option Must Be Selected**
   - One of four financing options (A, B, C, or D) MUST be checked
   - Cannot proceed without selection

5. **Termination Upon Ratification**
   - Escalation clause automatically terminates once final price is agreed
   - No further escalations after counter-offer acceptance

## STRATEGIC USE CASES

### When to Use This Addendum

✅ **Multiple Offer Situations** — Competitive markets with several buyers  
✅ **Price Justification** — Need documentation of market value  
✅ **Cash Buyer Advantage** — Seller prefers certainty of escalating to higher price  
✅ **Perceived Underpricing** — Buyer confident property will receive competing offers  

### When NOT to Use This Addendum

❌ **Single Offer Situation** — No competing offers expected  
❌ **Tight Market Control** — Buyer wants fixed, non-escalating price  
❌ **Financing Constraints** — Cannot accommodate potential cash requirements  
❌ **Below-Market Offer** — Unlikely other offers will exceed initial bid
