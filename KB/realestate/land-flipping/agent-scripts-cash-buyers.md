## STRATEGY: Finding Cash Buyers via Real Estate Agents

### Overview
**Core Concept**: Leverage local real estate agents who represent active fix-and-flip investors in your target market. These agents have direct relationships with cash buyers actively investing in the same area.

**Why This Works**: Fix-and-flippers prefer repeat deals in familiar neighborhoods; agents representing flippers are gatekeepers to qualified cash buyers.

---

## PROCESS: Identifying & Contacting Agent Representatives

### Step 1: Identify Flipped Properties
1. Visit **Zillow** or **Redfin**
2. Search for:
   - Recently sold homes in target area
   - Active listings in target area
3. Identify properties that are flips by looking for:
   - Highest-priced sales in area
   - Listing descriptions: "completely remodeled," "recently updated"
   - Photos showing new/renovated condition

### Step 2: Find Listing Agent Contact
1. Locate "Listed by" or "Presented by" section on property listing
2. Note: Agent name/brokerage may be minimal—google if needed
3. Obtain agent's phone number
4. **Note**: Agent info is always present on listing page

### Step 3: Make Initial Contact
- Call the agent representing the flipper
- Be prepared with agent script (see below)

---

## SCRIPT: Agent Contact Call

> "I'm a real estate investor and I have a property right now that I have under contract to purchase. It's a great fix and flip deal in the _________area and the reason why I'm calling you is because I see you listed a property for a flipper nearby on ______ street. Is that right?"

**[Wait for confirmation]**

> "Ok good, well I've got a lot going on right now…It looks like I might pick up a few more deals and so I want to just let this one go to another investor and I'm wondering if your flipper might be looking for another deal right now and would want to take a look at it."

**[Wait for response]**

> "Ok, perfect. Its scheduled to close on ______ date and I would just do simple assignment of contract to your investor and he can take it over. I'll text you the address and price and all the info and if he's interested, let me know and we can get him inside."

### Follow-up Contingency
If initial flipper not interested:

> "BTW - if this flipper isn't interested, do you know any other investors that might be looking for deals? Ok, thanks, let me know and I'll follow up with you tomorrow."

## NOTES FOR IMPLEMENTATION

- **Target Market**: Same geographic area as your deal
- **Buyer Profile**: Active fix-and-flip investors (best fit for investment properties)
- **Frequency**: Can pursue "a few more deals" (implies multiple deal pipeline)
- **Contact Method**: Direct phone call (agent response verification)
- **Timeline Clarity**: Must state scheduled closing date upfront
