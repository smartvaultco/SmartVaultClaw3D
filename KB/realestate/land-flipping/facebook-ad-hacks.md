## Overview
Facebook Lead Ads are a cost-effective platform for generating motivated seller leads for real estate investment. Leads typically cost $20-$40 per lead (vs. $400+ on Google Search). Facebook users who click ads are pre-qualified as motivated to sell quickly.

---

## Key Strategies & Benefits

### Why Facebook Ads Work for RE Investors
- **Self-qualifying leads**: Users who click have already raised their hand indicating interest in selling at a discount
- **Speed factor**: Online ad responders are typically desperate to sell immediately (vs. direct mail/cold calling where you filter through many unqualified leads)
- **Authority building**: Running ads positions you as a local market authority
- **Low barrier to entry**: User-friendly Ads Manager allows non-technical users to launch campaigns in hours

### Cost Comparison
- Facebook Lead Ads: $20-$40 CPL
- Google Search Ads: $400+ CPL

## Critical Compliance Notes

### Special Ad Categories – MANDATORY
Per Facebook's post-Cambridge Analytica policies:

> When setting up your campaign, you MUST mark your ads as "Housing" under "Special Ad Categories." If you don't do this, your ad may not get approved.

### Targeting Restrictions
As a "Housing" ad, **you are NOT allowed to exclude anyone by age, gender, or other demographics.**

### Date Caveat
> Facebook regularly updates their Ads Manager and ad policies. The content in this guide is accurate as of October 30, 2020. Always consult Facebook's support articles for the most recent information on ad policies and ad setup instructions.

## Targeting Strategy

### Recommended Approach
- Target by **zip code, city, or county** (broad targeting)
- Facebook's algorithm identifies users most likely to click within those locations
- Trust Facebook's optimization—they benefit from getting you the best leads at best price

### Advanced: Custom Audience Upload
- Requires minimum 25,000 leads
- Facebook matches against user profiles using name, phone, email
- If list smaller than 25K, use geographic targeting instead

### Exclusion Strategy
- You can exclude specific zip codes you don't want to invest in
- Important for avoiding markets outside your investment thesis

### Placement Options
- **Best performers**: Facebook News Feed
- **Avoid/test minimally**: Instagram, Stories, In-Stream videos, Search (budget waste risk)
- Can toggle individual placements on/off

## Step-by-Step: Facebook Lead Ad Setup

### Step 1: Create Campaign
1. Open Facebook Ads Manager
2. Click **Create**
3. Select **"Lead Generation"** as Campaign Objective
4. Name campaign using memorable convention, e.g.: `Lead Ad – Denver CO`
5. Name Ad Set and Ad accordingly

### Step 2: Setup Campaign
1. Click the "Off" button next to **Special Ad Categories** to turn it **ON**
2. Select **"Housing"** from dropdown
3. Keep default settings for rest of campaign
4. Click **Next**

### Step 3: Setup Ad Set
1. Choose your Facebook Business Page from **Page dropdown**
2. Set **Daily Budget**
3. Click **Next** (will proceed to audience step)

### Step 4: Create Your Audience
1. **Option A (if you have Custom Audience)**: Select it from dropdown
2. **Option B (geographic targeting)**: Enter zip code, city, or county
3. **Advanced**: Use "Drop Pin" on map and choose radius from that location
4. **Exclusions**: Repeat process for any locations to exclude
5. **Save for reuse**: Click "Save This Audience" if you'll target same area again

### Step 5: Finish Ad Set – Manual Placements
1. Click **Manual Placements**
2. Select placements (at minimum, select **Facebook News Feed**)
3. Consider removing **Instagram** if on tight budget (lower conversion)
4. Test other placements but monitor CPL
5. Keep default for Optimization & Delivery
6. Click **Next**

### Step 6: Create Your Ad
1. Connect Instagram account (if running ads there)
2. Under **Ad Setup**, select **"Single Image or Video"**
3. Upload your ad image
4. Set image dimensions: **1:1 ratio (square)** works best for most placements
5. Fill in ad text fields:
   - **Primary Text**
   - **Headline**
   - **Description**
   - **Display Link**
6. Monitor **Ad Preview** window as you add text
7. Click **Instant Form** under Question Format
8. Proceed to Step 7

### Step 7: Create Your Lead Form
1. Click **Create Form** (if you haven't created one yet)
2. **Optional**: Add Greeting for more form text, or leave blank for simplicity
3. Select questions for leads to answer:
   - **Minimum fields**: Address, Name, Email, Phone
   - Can add additional fields as needed
4. To add fields: Click **+Add Category** and scroll options
5. Monitor preview window as selections update
6. Add link to **Privacy Policy**
7. Confirm **"thank you" step** messaging (shown after form completion)
8. **Advanced users**: Go to Settings to customize sharing options and field names
9. Click **Publish**

### Step 8: Publish Your Ad
1. Return to Instant Form dropdown
2. Select the form you just created
3. **Double-check**: Review entire ad for typos and errors
4. Click green **Publish** button (bottom right)

### Expected Timeline
- Ad approval and go-live: **Within a couple hours**

## Post-Launch: Monitoring & Optimization

### Monitor Performance
1. Use **Ads Manager** to track campaigns
2. Watch **Cost Per Lead (CPL)**
3. If performance is weak, test:
   - New **ad copy**
   - New **images**
   - New **geographic locations**

### Access & Export Leads
1. Go to **Ad tab** in Ads Manager
2. Click **"On-Facebook Lead"** in Results column
3. View leads in **Leads Center** (on business page)
4. **Option A**: Download as spreadsheet
5. **Option B (Advanced)**: Use **Zapier** to automatically add Facebook leads to CRM in real-time
6. Upload leads into your CRM system
