## Legal Clauses & Contract Language

### Core Agreement Language

> "This Agreement is entered into this _____ day of _______________, 20 ___, by and between the undersigned parties. Be it resolved that in consideration of this recitals contained herein and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the undersigned parties hereby agree and certify that:"

### Clause 1: Non-Circumvention & Payment Obligation

> "They intend to be legally bound hereby irrevocably and agree not to circumvent, avoid payment of fees or commissions, avoid, bypass or obviate each other, directly or indirectly, in any transaction with any entity, partnership, individual, or trust, revealed by either party to the other, in conjunction with any project and/or transaction involving any real estate properties, contracts, agreements, options, or assignments thereof."

**Application:** Prevents parties from cutting each other out of deals or bypassing fee/commission agreements.

### Clause 2: Non-Disclosure Requirements

> "Nor shall either party disclose or otherwise reveal, to any third party, any confidential information revealed by the other, particularly that concerning partners, investors, lenders, buyers, organizations or individuals' names, addresses, emails, phone numbers, or any other means of access thereto, without the specific formal written consent of the other."

**Protected Information Categories:**
- Partners' names and contact details
- Investor identities
- Lender information
- Buyer details
- Any means of access to parties

### Clause 3: Liability & Penalties for Violation

> "It is further agreed that in the event either party shall at any time violate the terms of this Non-Disclosure and Non-Circumvention Agreement, such defaulting party shall be liable to pay to the other party any and all profits, and/or commissions which such non-defaulting party would have earned, or made from any transaction which may be consummated, as a result of, or through such unpermitted circumvention, plus all court costs and legal fees expended in the enforcement of this agreement."

**Consequence Structure:**
- Lost profits from circumvented transaction
- All commissions the non-defaulting party would have earned
- Court costs
- Legal fees for enforcement

### Clause 4: Term & Renewal

> "It is agreed that this Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of five (5) years or the life of any agreement, contracts, or commercial agreements, whichever is longer, and will automatically renew itself thereafter from year to year unless mutually agreed to in writing to cancel same within thirty days of the anniversary date of this Agreement."

**Key Timeline Points:**
- **Minimum Duration:** 5 years
- **Trigger:** Life of underlying agreements (whichever extends longer)
- **Auto-Renewal:** Year-to-year after initial term
- **Cancellation Window:** 30 days before anniversary date
- **Requirement:** Written mutual agreement to cancel

### Clause 5: Binding Effect & Successors

> "All the terms of this Agreement shall be binding and inure to the benefit of the parties hereto, their respective heirs, representatives, successors, assigns and designees."

**Coverage:** Agreement binds and benefits:
- Original parties
- Heirs
- Representatives
- Successors
- Assigns
- Designees

### Execution & Jurisdiction Section

> "This Agreement shall be interpreted under the laws of the State of ____________________. The parties have executed and delivered this Agreement and is effective from the date of signing by both or all parties."

**Fillable Fields:**
- State for governing law
- Effective date (upon dual signature)

## Best Practices for Implementation

### Before Using This Template

1. **Consult State Attorney** - Verify enforceability in applicable jurisdiction
2. **Customize All Blanks** - Never leave placeholder fields unfilled
3. **Identify Parties Clearly** - Use full legal names and titles
4. **Specify Effective Date** - Document exact execution date
5. **Review State Laws** - Confirm non-circumvention clauses are enforceable in target state

### Documentation Requirements

- **Signatures:** Both/all parties must sign
- **Witness:** Consider notarization for additional enforceability
- **Copies:** Each party retains executed original
- **Filing:** Consider recording if required by state law
