Step 5 Sending Your Contract

Contract

This bulletproof contract is one of the most important aspects of the wholesale transaction and is to be used between you and the Seller(s) of the property. This contract is to be provided to the title company or attorney that will oversee the closing.

**Never share this contract with your Cash Buyer(s). Your Cash Buyer(s) are to receive the Assignment Contract.
