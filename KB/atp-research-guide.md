## WHAT TO CAPTURE FROM EACH SEARCH

For each keyword, AnswerThePublic generates 5 categories. Capture ALL of these:

### 1. QUESTIONS (Gold Mine — these become ebook chapters)
- **What** questions → Definitions, explainers
- **How** questions → Tutorials, step-by-step guides
- **Why** questions → Persuasion content, objection handling
- **Can** questions → Myth-busting, capability content
- **Which** questions → Comparison content, tool reviews

### 2. PREPOSITIONS
- "AI automation **for** [industry]" → Niche targeting angles
- "AI marketing **with** [tool]" → Integration content
- "AI advertising **without** [objection]" → Objection-handling content

### 3. COMPARISONS
- "AI automation **vs** hiring" → Decision-making content
- "ChatGPT **vs** Claude **for** business" → Tool comparison content

### 4. ALPHABETICAL
- Long-tail variations you'd never think of
- Capture anything with high relevance to your services

### 5. RELATED SEARCHES
- Adjacent topics that expand your content map

## SELECTING THE TOP 10 PROMPTS/QUESTIONS

After collecting all data, rank by:

1. **Frequency** — Does this question appear across multiple platforms?
2. **Commercial Intent** — Does answering this lead to someone buying your service?
3. **Pain Intensity** — Is this a "hair on fire" problem or a "nice to know"?
4. **Your Authority** — Can you answer this better than anyone because of your systems/experience?
5. **Content Leverage** — Can this answer become a chapter AND a course module AND an ad angle?
