## THE BULLET SYSTEM — Liquidation Prevention

### What is Liquidation?
When price falls to your liquidation price → position auto-closes → you lose your entire initial investment. The protocol does this to protect the lenders.

### How Bullets Work
Adding capital to your position lowers the liquidation price significantly.

**Example with $5,000 initial investment at BTC $29,000:**
| Action | BTC Price | Liquidation Price | Safety Level |
|--------|-----------|------------------|--------------|
| Open position | $29,000 | ~$19,330 | Medium |
| Add Bullet 1 ($5K) at $23K | $23,000 | ~$13,629 | Safe |
| Add Bullet 2 ($5K) at lower level | Lower | ~$7,000 | Near impossible |

**Rules:**
- Pre-decide your bullet trigger prices BEFORE opening the position
- Don't wait until you're scared — decide in advance, execute automatically
- Bullets kept in BTC/ETH also appreciate = double benefit

## LIQUIDITY POOLS — Passive Income Layer

### What It Is
You deposit two assets into a pool (e.g., ETH + USDC). Traders use your liquidity to trade. You earn a % of every trade as fees.

### Why It's Powerful
- **No liquidation risk** (unlike leverage trading)
- Currently earning **100-116% APY** on certain pairs
- When crypto market heats up → more trading volume → higher yields (can hit 1000%+ APY)
- You are literally becoming the bank/exchange

### Risk: Impermanent Loss
If one asset moves much more than the other, you can end up with less value than just holding. Mitigated by:
- Using correlated pairs (ETH + stETH)
- Using stablecoin pairs (USDC + USDT) for zero impermanent loss, lower yield
