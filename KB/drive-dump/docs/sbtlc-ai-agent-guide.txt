## How to Access Your Agents


### Option 1: Web Browser (Recommended)
1. Open your browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, etc.)
2. Go to: **sb-trust-legal.vercel.app**
3. Enter the Gateway URL and Token (provided separately)
4. Click **Connect**
5. You'll see the office with your agents


### Option 2: Local Access
1. If running locally, open: **http://localhost:3000**
2. Enter Gateway Token when prompted
3. Click **Connect**


---


## How to Chat with an Agent


1. Click on any agent's avatar in the office
2. Click the **CHAT** button (bottom right corner)
3. Type your question or request
4. The agent will respond based on its specialization and knowledge base


### Tips for Best Results:
- **Be specific** — include names, amounts, dates, and jurisdictions
- **One topic per message** — don't combine multiple questions
- **Provide facts first** — the agents analyze before drafting
- **Ask for what you need** — "Draft a deed" vs "Analyze this situation"

## What to Ask Each Agent


### SB Trust Instrument Engine
- "Create a promissory note for [amount] from [maker] to [payee]"
- "Check if this instrument is negotiable"
- "Draft a negotiable instrument with these terms..."
- "Review this note for defects"


### Ma'at (Debt Remedy)
- "Analyze this debt strategy for legality and risk"
- "How can I dispute a debt under FDCPA/FCRA?"
- "Create a compliant debt validation letter"
- "What are lawful ways to reduce or discharge this debt?"


### Exclusive Equity
- "Analyze this quiet title situation"
- "Draft a TRO motion for [situation]"
- "What equitable remedies apply to [facts]?"
- "Prepare an accounting demand"


### Deeds & Land Patents
- "Draft a full Roark-style deed of conveyance"
- "Create a complete document package for [property]"
- "Draft an affidavit of status and authority"
- "Prepare a memorandum of trust"


### Trust
- "What type of trust do I need for [objective]?"
- "Draft a revocable living trust"
- "Create a land trust for [property]"
- "Analyze whether a 508(c)(1)(A) trust fits this situation"

## How to Update Agent Knowledge


### Updating Agent Instructions
1. Click on the agent's avatar
2. Click on the tabs on the left side:
   - **Identity** — Name and role
   - **Soul** — Personality and boundaries
   - **Agents** — Operating instructions and rules
   - **User** — Your info and preferences
   - **Tools** — Frameworks and templates
   - **Memory** — Knowledge base content
3. Edit the content
4. Click **Save**


### Adding New Documents to Knowledge Base
Contact your administrator (Luke) to run the KB ingestion script with new PDFs.

## Important Notes


- These agents do NOT provide legal advice as a licensed attorney

- Agents will refuse to draft fraudulent or illegal documents
- All instruments should be reviewed before use
- Agents flag risks and defects — pay attention to their warnings
