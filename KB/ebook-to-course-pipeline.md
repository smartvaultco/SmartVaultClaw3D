# Ebook-to-Course Pipeline — Smart Vault HQ
## Step 3: Turn Top 10 Questions into an Ebook + Course

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-12
**Purpose:** Repeatable system for turning research into two sellable products simultaneously

---

## THE CONCEPT

**One research session = Two products.**

Each of your Top 10 questions from AnswerThePublic becomes:
- **1 Ebook Chapter** (written long-form answer, 2,000-4,000 words)
- **1 Course Module** (video lesson + worksheet + action items)

The ebook is the tripwire ($19-$49). The course is the core product ($97-$497).

---

## EBOOK STRUCTURE

### Title: "The AI Growth Playbook: How Smart Business Owners Use AI to Get More Leads, Save 20+ Hours a Week, and Scale Without Hiring"
*(Adjust based on actual ATP research findings)*

### Format
- **Length:** 25,000-40,000 words (10 chapters x 2,500-4,000 words)

- **Design:** Clean, professional, branded with Smart Vault HQ identity

```

### Front Matter
1. **Cover Page** — Title, subtitle, author, Smart Vault HQ branding
2. **About the Author** — CJ's story, credentials, what makes him different
3. **Who This Book Is For** — SMB owners tired of wasting time/money

### Back Matter
1. **Resource List** — All tools mentioned, with links
2. **Free Audit CTA** — "Want us to build this for you? Book your free 15-min AI Audit at [link]"
3. **Course Upsell** — "Go deeper with the full AI Growth Masterclass — [link]"

## COURSE STRUCTURE

### Title: "The AI Growth Masterclass: Build Your Automated Business in 90 Days"

### Format
- **10 Modules** (1 per ebook chapter)
- **Each Module:** 15-30 min video lesson + PDF worksheet + action checklist
- **Bonus Module:** "The Smart Vault Method" (how to build your own NotebookLM knowledge base)
- **Platform:** Hosted on GHL (no extra subscription)

### Module Template (Use for Every Module)

```
MODULE [#]: [SAME TITLE AS EBOOK CHAPTER]

LESSON VIDEO (15-30 min)
- Intro: State the problem (2 min)
- Framework: Teach the system (8-15 min)
- Demo: Show it live — screen share, walkthrough (5-10 min)
- Recap + Next Steps (2 min)

PDF WORKSHEET
- Key concepts summary (1 page)
- Fill-in-the-blank exercises
- "Apply to YOUR business" prompts

ACTION CHECKLIST

- [ ] Step 2: [Specific action]
- [ ] Step 3: [Specific action]
- [ ] Deadline: Complete before next module

RESOURCE LINKS
- Tools mentioned in lesson
- Templates/swipe files (if applicable)
