## The Diamond Standard AI Growth Model

Reverse-engineer $1M/month revenue using AI-powered advertising systems. The model integrates two core concepts:

- **Smart Vault** — A personalized NotebookLM knowledge base that stores all research, audience data, and campaign intelligence in one queryable system.
- **"Multitude of Counselors"** — Cross-reference insights across multiple LLMs (Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, ChatGPT, Grock) to eliminate blind spots and validate strategy from every angle.

### New Standard Ad Framework

- **Thumb-Stopper Hook** — First 6-7 seconds must arrest the scroll
- **Problem Agitation** — Amplify the pain point immediately after hook
- **Solution Proof + CTA** — Deliver proof and call-to-action within 30-45 seconds total runtime

## Strategic Phases

### Phase 1: Articulate Business Foundation

Define mission, values, target audience, and revenue model with precision. Example pricing structure: $5K setup + $2,250/mo retainer. Stack: GoHighLevel + Hyros for tracking and fulfillment.

### Phase 2: Deep Research "Shadow Work"

Instruct AI to give uncomfortable truths. Push every LLM to challenge assumptions, pricing, positioning, and targeting. This is where weak strategies die before they cost real money.

### Phase 3: External Data Collection

- **AnswerThePublic.com** — Keywords, CPC data, search intent
- **Instagram / TikTok / YouTube** — Hashtag research and trending content
- **Amazon** — Gap analysis for underserved markets
- **Facebook Ad Library / TikTok Ad Library** — Competitor creative intelligence

### Phase 4: Populate Smart Vault

Load ALL research into NotebookLM. Every data point, every competitor insight, every audience signal. The Vault becomes your single source of truth for campaign generation.

### Phase 5: Generate Targeting Parameters

Extract targeting parameters directly from the populated Smart Vault — audiences, interests, behaviors, lookalikes, and custom segments.
