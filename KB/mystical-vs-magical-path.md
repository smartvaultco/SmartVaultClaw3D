## The Mystical Path
- Finding truth/God INSIDE oneself, not externally. YOU become the tools you need to grow
- Change the world inside yourself (internal state) rather than external environment
- Practice: meditation (listening to God), prayer (talking to God), shadow work, mindfulness
- Mystics act as introverts, cut off from society. Make THEMSELVES into an amulet/talisman
- 4th Dimension = internal dream realm connecting all of us. Requires "mystic mind" to navigate
- Key figures: Mooji, Sadhguru, Osho, Joe Dispenza
- Source: 4D Ascension Manual

## The Magical Path
- Uses elements and tools OUTSIDE the body to manifest desires. "Spiritual hackers" hacking the Matrix
- Foundation: Wicca. Must master earth, air, fire, water, ether + work with ancestors
- Practice: spell work, altar work, full moon rituals, herbs on candles, crystals, amulets, wands, staffs
- Earth element + blood = most powerful form to anchor/pull desires from astral plane
- Deities carry external weapons (axes, hammers, staffs, vajra) = practitioners of magic

## Psychedelic Soul Trips
- Soul trips = going deep inside own soul to repair spiritual damage/wipe away darkness
- Plant medicines: peyote, ayahuasca, mushrooms. Guided by Shaman with drum or spirit animal

## Future: Technological Magic & Quantum Body
- Traditional herbs losing potency (GMOs, pharmaceutical laws). Physical elements losing "umph"
- Must learn "new technological magic" and build "quantum body"
- Use internal energies to manifest, bypassing need for full moons or physical herbs
- Key figure: Brother Joseph. Source: Building the Quantum Body, The Mystical Mind Conference
