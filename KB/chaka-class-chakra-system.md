## The 7 Chakras

### 1. Root Chakra (Muladhara)
- **Location**: Base of spine / perineum
- **Element**: Earth
- **Color**: Red / Black
- **Glands**: Adrenals
- **Function**: Self-preservation, grounding, survival instinct, fight-or-flight
- **Balanced**: Grounded, secure, stable, connected to physical body
- **Overactive**: Greed, hoarding, materialism, aggression, fear-driven control
- **Underactive**: Disconnected, spacey, fearful, lacking stability, financial anxiety

### 2. Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)
- **Location**: Below navel / lower abdomen
- **Element**: Water
- **Color**: Orange
- **Glands**: Ovaries / Testes
- **Function**: Emotional identity, sexuality, creativity, childhood trauma storage
- **Balanced**: Emotionally fluid, creative, healthy sexuality, joy
- **Overactive**: Emotional volatility, sexual addiction, codependency, manipulation
- **Underactive**: Emotional numbness, guilt, low libido, creative blocks, repression

### 3. Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)
- **Location**: Above navel / stomach area
- **Element**: Fire
- **Color**: Yellow
- **Glands**: Pancreas
- **Function**: Personal power, will, ego, self-esteem, digestion
- **Balanced**: Confident, decisive, strong will, healthy boundaries
- **Overactive**: Domineering, controlling, anger, perfectionism, narcissism
- **Underactive**: Low self-esteem, passive, indecisive, victim mentality, digestive issues

### 4. Heart Chakra (Anahata)
- **Location**: Center of chest
- **Element**: 4th Ether (Air)
- **Color**: Green / Pink
- **Glands**: Thymus
- **Function**: Gateway to higher realms, unconditional love, "wounded child" healing center
- **Balanced**: Compassionate, loving, empathetic, forgiving, emotionally open
- **Overactive**: People-pleasing, codependency, jealousy, boundary-less giving
- **Underactive**: Cold, distant, bitter, unable to forgive, heart disease risk

### 5. Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)
- **Location**: Throat
- **Element**: 3rd Ether
- **Color**: Blue
- **Glands**: Thyroid / Parathyroid
- **Function**: Communication, self-expression, truth-speaking, "The Word" (creative power of speech)
- **Balanced**: Clear communicator, truthful, good listener, creative expression
- **Overactive**: Talking too much, gossiping, inability to listen, verbal aggression
- **Underactive**: Fear of speaking, secretive, poor communication, thyroid issues, sore throat

### 6. Brow / Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)
- **Location**: Center of forehead
- **Element**: 2nd Ether
- **Color**: Indigo
- **Glands**: Pineal
- **Function**: Intuition, spiritual sight, clairvoyance, inner vision, dream recall
- **Balanced**: Strong intuition, vivid dreams, clear visualization, spiritual awareness
- **Overactive**: Hallucinations, delusions, nightmares, psychic overwhelm
- **Underactive**: Poor intuition, lack of imagination, denial of spiritual reality, calcified pineal

### 7. Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)
- **Location**: Top of head
- **Element**: 1st Ether
- **Color**: Violet / White
- **Glands**: Pituitary
- **Function**: Pure conscious awareness, God connection, cosmic unity, enlightenment
- **Balanced**: Spiritual connection, universal understanding, presence, bliss
- **Overactive**: Spiritual addiction, dissociation from physical reality, "God complex"
- **Underactive**: Spiritual disconnection, materialism, closed-mindedness, existential depression

### Transcendental Chakras
- **Soul Star**: Above crown — higher self connection, akashic records access
- **Spiritual Heart**: Higher octave of heart — divine love beyond personal emotion
- **Earth Star**: Below feet — deep earth grounding, ancestral connection
- **Earth Gateway**: Deepest grounding point — planetary consciousness link
