## Business Validation Frameworks

### The 2-20-200 Framework
1. **2 hours:** Online research (Reddit, keyword tools) to gauge demand
2. **20 hours:** Build landing page + have direct conversations with potential customers
3. **200 hours:** Build the product ONLY after demand is proven

### Post, Rent, Buy (PRB)
Eliminate financial risk completely:
1. Post an item on marketplace BEFORE you own it
2. Rent/sell it to a customer
3. ONLY THEN buy the inventory to fulfill

### Permissionless Projects (Canvas Strategy)
Do the work for a dream client BEFORE asking permission. Identify a gap, solve it (redesign pitch deck, convert book to new format), send it free to secure high-ticket retainers.

### The Trojan Horse Strategy
Don't sell massive operational overhauls. Sell a small, specific, high-impact automation (missed-call text-back) to get foot in door, then upsell larger retainers.

### The Stair-Step Method
If you can't code, don't build a massive SaaS. Build a simple plugin/add-on inside an existing ecosystem (Shopify app, Zoom integration) where the marketplace handles marketing.

## High-ROI Business Models

### Programmatic SEO & Niche Directories
- AI-generated databases (dog parks, high-protein snacks, plasma donation centers)
- Capture long-tail organic search traffic
- Monetize: ads, affiliate links, selling leads to listed businesses

### AI Automation & Implementation Agencies
- Local businesses drowning in fragmented software
- White-label GoHighLevel to replace legacy systems
- Deploy AI agents for customer service, invoicing, outbound lead gen
- Charge: $2,000-$3,000 setup + monthly maintenance retainer

### Low-Content Publishing (KDP & Printables)
- AI-generated coloring books, journals, RV logs on Amazon KDP or Etsy
- Find high-search-volume, low-competition keywords
- Adapt proven titles

### The "Sweaty Startup" (Blue-Collar Services)
- Low competition from tech entrepreneurs
- Examples: stump grinding, commercial HVAC coil cleaning, drone roof/facade inspections

### Paid Newsletters & Media Companies
- Hyper-niche or localized email lists (city-specific daily digest)
- Own your audience without algorithmic social media
- Monetize: local sponsorships, affiliate networks (SparkLoop), digital products

## Marketing & Sales Strategies

### Reverse Engineering Competitors
- Use **Meta Ads Library** to find longest-running competitor ads (= profitable)
- Use **Wayback Machine** to track pricing/landing page evolution
- Don't reinvent the wheel — copy what's making money

### AIDA Copywriting Formula
- **A**ttention (hooks/facts)
- **I**nterest (testimonials/use cases)
- **D**esire (how their life improves)
- **A**ction (how to buy)

### Frank Kern's 8-Step Sales Process
1. Overwhelm with features/value
2. Establish high perceived value
3. Add bonuses
4. Drop the price significantly
5. Explain why the price is so low
6. Introduce bold guarantee (Risk Reversal)
7. Add urgency/scarcity

### Facebook Ads for Scale
- Use "Advantage+" broad targeting
- Let ad creative do the targeting — algorithm finds buyers if hook is clear

## Key Hacks & Actionable Advice

- **Steal the Prompt:** Feed an AI output back into an LLM: "Generate a prompt that could have generated these exact examples." Use that reverse-engineered prompt.
- **Build Authority Databases:** Scrape Craigslist for niche repair shops, use Wisconsin Franchise Search for competitor financials. Asymmetric information = competitive advantage.
- **Monitor Competitors:** Use **Distill.io** for website change tracking, **GummySearch** to aggregate subreddit complaints
- **Record Everything:** AI wearable (Limitless pendant) or Fathom/Otter for meetings → feed into LLM for "second brain"
- **Sales First:** Don't get bogged in LLC formation or perfect websites before making a sale. Lead gen is the ONLY priority on day one.
