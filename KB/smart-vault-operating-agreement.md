# OPERATING AGREEMENT
## Smart Vault LLC
### A Michigan Limited Liability Company

---

**Effective Date:** March 31, 2026

This Operating Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by the undersigned Member of Smart Vault Co. LLC, a Michigan Limited Liability Company ("Company"), formed pursuant to the Michigan Limited Liability Company Act, MCL 450.4101 et seq.

---

## ARTICLE I — FORMATION

**1.1 Name.** The name of the Company is Smart Vault LLC.

**1.2 State of Organization.** The Company is organized under the laws of the State of Michigan.

**1.3 Principal Office.** The principal office of the Company shall be located at such place as the Member may designate from time to time.

**1.4 Registered Agent.** The Company shall maintain a registered agent in the State of Michigan as required by law.

**1.5 Purpose.** The Company is organized to engage in any lawful business activity permitted under Michigan law, including but not limited to: digital marketing services, AI automation services, software development, e-commerce, trading, digital product creation, real estate services, consulting, and any related activities.

**1.6 Term.** The Company shall continue perpetually unless dissolved in accordance with this Agreement or Michigan law.

---

## ARTICLE II — MEMBER

**2.1 Sole Member.** The Company has one (1) Member:

| Member Name | Ownership Interest |
|------------|-------------------|
| Cortaz Calhoun Jr. | 100% |

**2.2 Membership Interest.** The Member holds 100% of the membership interest in the Company.

**2.3 Additional Members.** Additional members may be admitted only upon the written consent of the existing Member.

---

## ARTICLE III — MANAGEMENT

**3.1 Member-Managed.** The Company shall be member-managed. The Member shall have full authority to manage and conduct the business and affairs of the Company.

**3.2 Authority of Member.** The Member has the authority to:
- Open and manage bank and brokerage accounts in the Company's name
- Enter into contracts and agreements on behalf of the Company
- Purchase, sell, lease, or otherwise deal in real and personal property
- Borrow money and execute promissory notes
- Hire and terminate employees and contractors
- Make all business decisions without requiring additional approval

**3.3 No Meetings Required.** As a single-member LLC, the Company is not required to hold formal meetings. Decisions of the Member may be made by written consent.

---

## ARTICLE IV — CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

**4.1 Initial Contribution.** The Member's initial capital contribution shall be recorded in the Company's books and records.

**4.2 Additional Contributions.** The Member may make additional capital contributions at any time at their sole discretion.

**4.3 No Required Contributions.** The Member is not required to make any additional capital contributions.

---

## ARTICLE V — ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS

**5.1 Allocations.** All profits and losses of the Company shall be allocated 100% to the Member.

**5.2 Distributions.** Distributions shall be made to the Member at such times and in such amounts as the Member determines, subject to applicable law.

**5.3 Tax Treatment.** The Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal income tax purposes (sole proprietorship taxation) unless the Member elects otherwise.

---

## ARTICLE VI — BANKING AND ACCOUNTS

**6.1 Company Accounts.** The Company shall maintain separate bank and financial accounts in the Company's name using the Company's EIN.

**6.2 Brokerage Accounts.** The Member is authorized to open brokerage and trading accounts (including but not limited to Tastytrade, Alpaca, Interactive Brokers, Coinbase, and similar platforms) in the name of Smart Vault Co. LLC.

**6.3 No Commingling.** Company funds shall not be commingled with the personal funds of the Member.

---

## ARTICLE VII — TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTEREST

**7.1 Transfer Restrictions.** The Member may not transfer their membership interest without complying with applicable Michigan law.

**7.2 Transfer to Trust.** The Member may transfer their membership interest to a trust of which the Member is the trustee and/or beneficiary without restriction.

---

## ARTICLE VIII — DISSOLUTION

**8.1 Dissolution Events.** The Company shall be dissolved upon:
- Written determination of the Member to dissolve
- Entry of a judicial decree of dissolution
- Any other event required by Michigan law

**8.2 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Member shall wind up the Company's affairs, pay all liabilities, and distribute remaining assets to the Member.

---

## ARTICLE IX — INDEMNIFICATION

**9.1 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify the Member and any agents of the Company to the fullest extent permitted by Michigan law against claims arising from Company business.

---

## ARTICLE X — MISCELLANEOUS

**10.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Michigan.

**10.2 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement of the Member with respect to the subject matter hereof.

**10.3 Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by the Member.

**10.4 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.
