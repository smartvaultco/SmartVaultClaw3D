## 4. Sales Frameworks

### Show, Don't Tell (Self-Selling Demo)
- NO pitch decks — business owners have "AI fatigue"
- Send demo link to Smart Website branded to their niche
- They call the AI Voice Agent or chat the bot → AI sells itself

### Instant Pain Point Method
- Offer 14-day free trial of AI Smart Website/Voice Agent
- During trial, AI handles their calls/leads
- Trial ends → AI turns off → they feel pain of losing leads
- Close: "How many leads are you willing to lose if I don't keep this on?"

### ELF Process
Explanation → Lead Magnet → Free Trial = core cold outreach → client conversion sequence

### GGIP AI Prompting Framework
Goal, Info, Personality — keep prompts short to avoid confusing the LLM
