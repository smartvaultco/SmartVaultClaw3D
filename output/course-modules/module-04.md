### TEACH (10 minutes)

**[SLIDE: "The Cost of Silence" -- stats on screen]**

First, let us talk about what inconsistency is costing you right now.

Companies that post consistently generate 67 percent more leads per month. Email marketing delivers 36 dollars for every dollar spent. But here is the number that should bother you: when you post a burst of content one week and then go silent for three weeks, you are training your audience to forget you. The algorithms punish that. Your followers disengage. Open rates decay. And every single day you are not visible, a competitor fills that gap.

The old solution? Hire a content team. Social media manager -- 55 to 75K. Copywriter -- 50 to 70K. Video editor -- 45 to 65K. You are looking at 150 to 210 thousand dollars a year in salary alone. Plus benefits. Plus management overhead. Plus the three months it takes to onboard them to your voice.

The new solution costs you one hour per week and about 200 dollars a month.

**[SLIDE: "The Content Creation Engine -- SOP-002" with the framework diagram]**

Here is the framework. One Pillar recording per week. That is a 20 to 45 minute video or audio where you talk about what you already know. Not something you have to research. Something a client asked you this week. A mistake you see people making. A process you have refined.

From that single recording, AI generates:

**[SLIDE: The 30+ Content Pieces list -- animate each bullet]**

- One newsletter edition
- Five to seven social media posts, optimized per platform
- One email sequence of three to five emails
- One long-form blog post, 1,500 to 2,500 words
- Eight to twelve micro-content clips, 15 to 60 seconds each
- One set of quote graphics

That is 30-plus pieces. Enough to post twice a day across all your platforms for two full weeks. From one sitting.

**[SLIDE: "Step-by-Step Breakdown" header]**

Let me walk you through the six steps.

**[SLIDE: Step 1 -- Record Your Pillar Content]**

Step 1: Record your Pillar content. 30 to 45 minutes. Pick one topic. Use a three to five bullet outline -- do not script it word for word. Your audience wants your real perspective, not polished corporate copy. Record on your phone with a tripod, use Zoom, or if you want higher quality, Riverside.fm or Descript. Record both video and audio -- video gives you the most repurposing options.

**[SLIDE: Step 2 -- Transcribe and Extract]**

Step 2: Transcribe and extract. Upload to Descript, Otter.ai, or any transcription tool. Then feed that transcript into your LLM -- Claude, ChatGPT, or both. Use this prompt -- and I want you to save this prompt because you will use it every single week:

**[SLIDE: Show the master prompt on screen]**

"You are a content strategist. I am going to give you a transcript of a 30-minute recording. Extract: the three to five main points, the single most compelling insight, any quotable one-liners, a suggested blog post outline, seven social media post angles, and a three-email sequence outline. Audience: describe your audience. Tone: describe your voice."

That prompt does the heavy lifting. Save it. Customize it for your business. Use it every week.

**[SLIDE: Step 3 -- Generate Your Content Stack]**

Step 3: Generate your content stack. Blog post -- have the AI write a full 1,500 to 2,500 word post using your transcript as source material. This is not generic AI content. It is YOUR content, structured by AI. Social media posts -- generate five to seven posts optimized per platform. LinkedIn gets longer story-driven posts. Instagram gets punchy captions. X gets single-insight threads. TikTok gets short-form video scripts.

Newsletter -- pull the core insight, add a personal intro, include a CTA. It should feel like a note from a friend. Email sequence -- three to five emails that feed into your lead nurture system. Micro-content -- use Descript or OpusClip to auto-detect the best 15 to 60 second clips from your recording. AI finds the moments with the most energy and the clearest insights.

**[SLIDE: Step 4 -- "HUMAN REVIEW: NON-NEGOTIABLE" in bold red]**

Step 4: Human review. I need you to hear this clearly. AI drafts. Humans approve. Every single piece of content goes through a human review before it publishes. Period. This is not optional.

Why? Three reasons. One -- accuracy. AI hallucinate statistics. It misattributes quotes. It confidently states things that are wrong. Your reputation is on the line. Two -- voice. AI gets close, but close is not good enough. You need to read every piece and ask: would I actually say this? Three -- legal liability. Unsubstantiated claims about results or income create real legal exposure. A five-minute review catches problems a lawsuit will not forgive.

Block 60 to 90 minutes after your content stack is generated. Read everything. Edit what needs editing. Kill what does not sound like you. Approve the rest.

**[SLIDE: Step 5 -- Schedule and Distribute]**

Step 5: Schedule and distribute. Load your approved content into Buffer, Hootsuite, or Later. Two posts per day across your active platforms. Set your email sequences in your CRM -- ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, GoHighLevel. Upload your blog post. Optimize for SEO with SurferSEO or Clearscope if you want organic traffic.

**[SLIDE: Step 6 -- Measure and Iterate]**

Step 6: Measure and iterate. At the end of each two-week cycle, look at what performed. Which posts got the most engagement? Which emails had the highest open rates? Feed those insights back into your next Pillar recording. The system gets smarter every cycle because you are getting smarter about what your audience wants.

**[SLIDE: Tool Stack with monthly costs]**

Quick note on tools. Your total monthly cost: 100 to 200 dollars a month. Recording with Riverside at 15 a month. Transcription with Descript at 24. AI with Claude Pro at 20. Canva Pro at 13. Buffer at 6 per channel. OpusClip at 19 if you want auto-clips. Compare that to one content hire.
