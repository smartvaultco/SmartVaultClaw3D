| **Leverage (DeFi)** | Borrowing from a liquidity pool to control a larger position. 3x leverage on $5K = control $15K. | ARCrypto |

| **Options (Puts)** | Right to SELL/force someone to buy at a specified price. Bearish strategy + insurance. | Jason Brown |
| **Cash-Secured Put (CSP)** | Selling a put while holding full cash to buy 100 shares if assigned. Collects premium upfront. | Wheel Strategy |
| **Covered Call (CC)** | Selling a call against 100 shares you own. Collects premium like "rent." | Wheel Strategy |

## 1.7 Warnings, Mistakes to Avoid & Risk Factors

### DeFi Leverage Risks
- NEVER use above 3x leverage (near-certain liquidation)
- Always have 2 bullets BEFORE opening a position
- Borrowing fees can reach 50%/yr -- only use leverage if expecting 150%+ gains
- Impermanent loss in LPs when assets move disproportionately
- Self-custody: keep funds in MetaMask, not on exchanges (exchange freeze risk)
- Crypto can draw down 50%+ -- only invest what you can hold through that

### Options Risks
- NEVER hold naked stock through earnings without put insurance (overnight gaps bypass stop-losses)
- No penny stocks under $5 -- "casino stocks heading toward bankruptcy"
- Shorting stock = UNLIMITED risk; use puts instead (capped risk)
- Don't refuse to close a 90%-up trade over $3 commission (Friday news can wipe gains)
- Iron condors: NEVER ride to expiration -- take profits when they appear

### Wheel Strategy Mistakes
1. Don't wheel stocks just for high premium (high premium = high risk)
2. Don't sell puts on stocks you wouldn't own 6+ months
3. Never sell covered calls below your cost basis (locks in guaranteed loss)
4. Don't hold through earnings (gaps wipe months of premium)
5. Don't wheel with money needed in < 12 months
6. Max 2 rolls; every roll must be net credit
7. Don't start with 10 positions -- start with 1-2
8. Don't ignore Greeks (delta, theta, IV)
9. Don't panic at assignment -- it's the strategy working
10. Don't compare to S&P in a raging bull (wheel trades income for reduced risk)

### Algorithmic Trading Risks
- Mean reversion is CATASTROPHIC when fading a real breakout -- ALWAYS use stops
- Never increase position size on a losing reversion trade
- Momentum has low win rate (35-45%) -- emotionally hard to handle losing streaks
- VWAP resets daily -- intraday only, not for swing trades

### Scam / Verification Red Flags
- "100% win rate" or "never loses"
- "Guaranteed returns" or "risk-free"
- Large upfront payment before revealing strategy
- "Proprietary AI" with no explanation
- Only shows winning trades / no drawdown data
- Pressure to deposit quickly
- CFTC warning: "AI trading bot" language used in Ponzi schemes
- Reddit/Twitter P&L screenshots are trivially fakeable
