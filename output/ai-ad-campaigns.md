# CREATIVE SPECS + TESTING PROTOCOL
# ═══════════════════════════════════════

## Image Specs (Meta)
- **Feed:** 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (4:5 portrait)
- **Stories/Reels:** 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- **Text overlay:** Keep under 20% of image area
- **Brand colors:** Navy #0A0E1A background, Cyan #00D4FF accent, Gold #FFD700 CTA, White text

## Video Specs
- **Meta Feed:** 1:1 or 4:5, 15-60 seconds
- **Reels/Stories:** 9:16, 15-60 seconds
- **YouTube Pre-Roll:** 16:9, 60-90 seconds
- **Captions:** Always on (85% of Meta video watched without sound)

## Testing Protocol (per New Standard framework)
1. **Week 1:** Launch all 5 lead magnet ads with $10/day each = $50/day total
2. **72-hour check:** Kill anything below 0.7% CTR or $0 leads
3. **Week 2:** Scale winners to $25-50/day, launch tripwire ads to warm audience
4. **Week 3:** Launch course ads to ebook buyers, begin retargeting
5. **Week 4:** Full funnel live — optimize based on CPA and ROAS

## Multitude of Counselors (from the playbook)
For each ad concept, generate 3 variations using different AI models:
1. **Claude** — strategic, data-driven copy
2. **ChatGPT** — conversational, accessible tone
3. **Perplexity** — research-backed, stat-heavy angle

Test all three. Let the data pick the winner.


## Workflow 2: Ebook Purchase
```

→ Tag "Ebook Buyer"

```
